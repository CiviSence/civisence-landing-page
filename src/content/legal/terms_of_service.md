## 6. Issue Reporting and Voting Rules

To ensure our demand-aggregation algorithms are accurate and useful for administrators:
* **Anti-Spam Cooldown:** You are restricted to submitting a maximum of three (3) new issue reports per twenty-four (24) hour period. We reserve the right to modify these rate limits via our caching layer.
* **No Duplicate Farming:** You shall not coordinate with groups to submit multiple duplicate reports for the same infrastructure failure. You should instead upvote the existing report to aggregate demand.
* **Fuzzy Normalization:** The platform automatically strips punctuation and spaces from suggested organization names and locations to identify matches. You agree not to attempt to bypass this matching normalization to create duplicate workspaces.
* **Upvoting and Downvoting Integrity:** You shall not use multiple accounts, scripts, bots, or voting groups to artificially manipulate the priority score (`support_score` or issue net score) of any reported issue or poll.
