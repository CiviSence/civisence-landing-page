# CiviSence Contact & Support Policy

**Version:** 0.0.0.1  
**Effective Date:** July 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026  

---

## 1. Scope of Support
CiviSence provides digital software support for our API services, mobile apps, and web portal dashboards. 

* **Software Support:** Covers account access issues, JWT login errors, bug reports, file upload failures on Cloudinary, push notification setup issues, or data deletion requests.
* **Physical Infrastructure Issues (Escalation Disclaimer):** CiviSence does not resolve physical maintenance issues (e.g. leaking taps, electrical short-circuits, damaged roadways). Physical resolution is the sole responsibility of your registered **Organization** (your college, school, neighborhood association, or municipal council). We do not send maintenance staff, dispatch technicians, or guarantee repair windows.

## 3. Support Response Timelines (SLA)
We strive to address support inquiries promptly. Our target response timelines are:

| Request Category | Target Verification/Response Time |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Account Access & Security** | 24 - 48 Hours |
| **Data Deletion / De-activation** | 48 Hours |
| **General Bug Reports / Diagnostics** | 3 Business Days |
| **Grievances & Legal Enquiries** | 5 Business Days |

---

## 4. User Obligations during Support
When interacting with our support staff, you agree to:
* Provide accurate account details, browser profiles, and error messages.
* **Never share your account password.** CiviSence support staff will never ask for your plain text password.
* Refrain from using abusive, threatening, or obscene language. We reserve the right to close support tickets immediately and ban users who harass support personnel.
