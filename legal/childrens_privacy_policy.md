# CiviSence Children's Privacy Policy

**Version:** 0.0.0.1  
**Effective Date:** July 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026  

---

## 1. Introduction
CiviSence is a civic issue reporting, demand-aggregation, and voting platform built primarily for educational institutions, schools, colleges, and local neighborhoods. In many instances, minors aged between thirteen (13) and seventeen (17) use the Platform. This Children's Privacy Policy describes the steps we take to protect young users, how we handle their personal profiles, and the rights of parents and legal guardians.

---

## 2. Age Restrictions
* **Under 13 Prohibited:** CiviSence is strictly prohibited for children under the age of thirteen (13). We do not knowingly collect, verify, or retain any personal profiles or data from children under 13. If you believe we have accidentally collected data from a child under 13, contact us immediately at [civisence@zohomail.in](mailto:civisence@zohomail.in) or [civisence@gmail.com](mailto:civisence@gmail.com).
* **Minors (13–17):** Users aged 13 to 17 are permitted to register an account and join school/college workspaces, subject to obtaining consent or supervision from their parent or legal guardian.

---

## 3. Data Collection and Usage for Minors
For users aged 13 to 17, we collect the same structural data as adult users to ensure functional operations, including:
* **Identification details:** Name, email address, password hash, and date of birth.
* **School/College Metadata:** Registration numbers, course directories, class years, department identifiers, and hostel room numbers.
* **Verification Documents:** Proof of enrollment (e.g. photo of student ID card) uploaded to request account verification.
* **Usage Records:** Issue reports, comments, upvotes, poll responses, and direct messages.

### 3.1 Processing Limitation
We process minor data strictly to:
* Validate enrollment in the educational Organization.
* Present issue reports to school administrators.
* Deliver push alerts via FCM.
* Track analytics internally without building commercial advertising profiles.

---

## 4. Visibility and Sharing Boundaries
CiviSence restricts minor profile exposure:
* **Workspace Isolation:** A minor's issue submissions, comments, and name are visible **only** to other verified members of that same specific school or college Organization. They are not indexed by public search engines (e.g. Google) or exposed to external advertisers.
* **Verification Document Access:** Document uploads (student IDs) are stored in secure buckets (Cloudinary) and are visible only to the Organization's verified Administrators for verification purposes. They are permanently deleted immediately upon review.

---

## 5. Parent and Legal Guardian Rights
Parents and legal guardians of users under the age of 18 hold full rights over their child's personal data. You can request to:
1. Review the personal information we hold about your child.
2. Edit or correct inaccurate registration and profile details.
3. Terminate your child's account and initiate the complete data deletion and anonymization workflow.
4. Refuse permission for further data processing.

To execute any of these requests:
* Email us at [civisence@zohomail.in](mailto:civisence@zohomail.in) or [civisence@gmail.com](mailto:civisence@gmail.com).
* Provide the child's registered email address, name, school affiliation, and proof of your status as their parent or legal guardian.
