# Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** July 8, 2026  
**Version:** 0.0.0.1  

Welcome to **CiviSence**. Our mission is to help communities, educational institutions, organizations, and local authorities identify, discuss, and resolve civic issues through respectful collaboration. These Community Guidelines explain the standards expected from everyone using our platform.

By using CiviSence, you agree to follow these guidelines.

---

## 1. Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect and professionalism.

You must not:
* Harass, threaten, or intimidate others.
* Use abusive, hateful, or discriminatory language.
* Engage in bullying or personal attacks.
* Encourage violence or illegal activities.
* Impersonate another person or organization.

Constructive disagreement is welcome, but personal attacks are not.

---

## 2. Report Genuine Civic Issues

CiviSence is intended for reporting legitimate issues that affect communities, campuses, organizations, or public spaces.

Examples include:
* Infrastructure damage
* Water leakage
* Electricity issues
* Waste management problems
* Road hazards
* Safety concerns
* Public facility maintenance
* Campus maintenance requests
* Community improvement suggestions

Do not submit false, misleading, or intentionally fabricated reports.

---

## 3. Provide Accurate Information

When creating reports:
* Use clear titles and descriptions.
* Upload genuine photos or videos when possible.
* Select the correct category.
* Provide accurate location details.
* Avoid exaggeration or misinformation.

Repeated submission of false reports may result in account restrictions.

---

## 4. Respect Privacy

Do not post personal or confidential information about yourself or others without permission.

Examples include:
* Phone numbers
* Email addresses
* Home addresses
* Government identification numbers
* Financial information
* Medical information
* Private conversations

If sensitive information is accidentally included, it may be removed by moderators.

---

## 5. Appropriate Media Uploads

You may upload images, videos, or supporting documents that relate directly to the reported issue.

Do not upload content that:
* Is unrelated to the report.
* Contains nudity or sexually explicit material.
* Promotes violence or graphic content.
* Contains malware or harmful files.
* Infringes copyright or intellectual property rights.
* Violates another person's privacy.

---

## 6. Respect Intellectual Property

Upload only content that:
* You created,
* You own, or
* You have permission to use.

By uploading content, you retain ownership while granting CiviSence a non-exclusive license to host, display, process, and distribute the content solely for operating and improving the platform.

---

## 7. Keep Discussions Constructive

Comments should contribute meaningfully to the discussion.

Avoid:
* Spam
* Repeated comments
* Advertising
* Off-topic discussions
* Political campaigning unrelated to the reported issue
* Personal attacks

Healthy discussion and constructive feedback are encouraged.

---

## 8. Messaging Responsibly

Private messaging should be used respectfully.

Do not use messaging to:
* Harass users
* Send spam
* Scam others
* Share malicious links
* Request sensitive personal information
* Promote illegal activities

Repeated misuse may result in messaging restrictions or account suspension.

---

## 9. No Spam or Manipulation

Users must not:
* Create fake accounts.
* Artificially inflate votes.
* Manipulate polls.
* Automate interactions using bots without authorization.
* Post repetitive or promotional content.
* Circumvent platform restrictions.

Accounts involved in manipulation may be suspended or permanently banned.

---

## 10. Voting Fairly

Voting is intended to reflect genuine community interest.

Do not:
* Coordinate fake voting campaigns.
* Use multiple accounts.
* Exchange votes for rewards.
* Manipulate issue rankings.

---

## 11. Illegal Activities

Content involving illegal activities is prohibited, including but not limited to:
* Fraud
* Identity theft
* Unauthorized access to systems
* Distribution of malware
* Terrorism or violent extremism
* Human exploitation
* Child exploitation
* Sale of illegal goods
* Drug trafficking
* Weapons trafficking

Such content may be removed immediately and reported to appropriate authorities where required by law.

---

## 12. Protect Children

Users under the minimum permitted age may not create accounts.

Do not:
* Target minors with inappropriate content.
* Exploit children.
* Share content that endangers minors.

Content involving child exploitation will be removed immediately and may be reported to relevant authorities.

---

## 13. Respect Organizations

Do not impersonate:
* Colleges
* Schools
* Municipal authorities
* Government departments
* Organizations
* Officials
* Administrators

Verified organizations may receive official verification through CiviSence.

---

## 14. Reporting Violations

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, use the in-app reporting tools.

Reports may include:
* Spam
* Harassment
* Hate speech
* False information
* Copyright infringement
* Offensive media
* Fake accounts
* Other policy violations

We review reports as quickly as reasonably possible.

---

## 15. Enforcement

Depending on the severity or frequency of violations, we may:
* Remove content.
* Hide content from public view.
* Limit account functionality.
* Suspend messaging privileges.
* Issue warnings.
* Temporarily suspend accounts.
* Permanently ban accounts.
* Remove organizations from the platform.
* Report illegal activities to relevant authorities where legally required.

Serious violations may result in immediate action without prior warning.

---

## 16. Appeals

If you believe your account or content was restricted in error, you may submit an appeal through our support channels.

Each appeal is reviewed individually.

Submitting repeated or abusive appeals may delay the review process.

---

## 17. Updates

These Community Guidelines may be updated periodically to reflect new features, legal requirements, or improvements to the platform.

The latest version will always be available within the app and on our website.

Continued use of CiviSence after updates indicates acceptance of the revised guidelines.

---
